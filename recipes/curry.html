Curry

A recipe for an imaginary curry

Ingredients

  • Curry Cube
  • Water or Chicken Stock
  • Potatos
  • Carrots
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Cubed meat

    • Steps

    1. Finely dice garlic and onions, sautee them on medium heat
    2. Add cubed meat, cook on medium/medium-high until outside is browned
    3. Boil water
    4. Peel and shop veges into 1 inch pieces
    5. Add curry cube and veges into water, stirring occasionally. Simmer for 15 mins
    6. Add meat, garlic, onion mix.
    7. Simmer for 5 minutes and serve
